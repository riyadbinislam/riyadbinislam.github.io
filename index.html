About Me

Recent M.S. in Data Science from Carolina University with a passion for computational cognitive science, interpretable ML, and understanding belief formation & decision-making. My capstone explored anomaly detection in border traffic data using clustering and LSTM forecasting. I'm applying these skills to interdisciplinary PhD work in cognitive science and neuroscience.

Outside code, I'm writing two book projects: Book of Belief (on how beliefs shape reality) and NeoGenesis (bridging consciousness and complex systems).
